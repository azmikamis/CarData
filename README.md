CarData
=======

Web browser and scraper for used car sites

Click on shortcut button to website of choice (Manheim/Ove)

At Manheim
1. Search for car
2. Click Get Manheim
3. Click Scrape AutoTrader
4. Click Scrape CarGuru

At Ove
1. Search for car
2. Click Basic View
2. Click Get Ove (1)
3. Click Detail View
4. Click Get Ove (2)
5. Click Scrape AutoTrader
6. Click Scrape CarGuru
